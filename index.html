D.A.V public school

campus

DAV Public School, Power Grid Campus Bihar Sharif, is a Senior Secondary School (XI-XII), affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The School is a Coed Day School, with classes from LKG to XII. It is an English, Hindi Medium school. The school is located in Power Grid Campus area of Bihar Sharif.
